About

Board Game QR is a prototype system that let's you log your game plays to Board Game Geek by scanning a QR code.

To use:

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Generate and print QR codes for your games. Try printing on sticker paper and sticking the QR code to your game box.
  3. Scan the QR code with your smartphone to log a play
